Go Dutch Account Deletion

Effective Date: 7 March 2026

We respect your right to delete your account at any time. The following instructions explain how to permanently remove your account and all associated personal data.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Go Dutch app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Menu via the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and select Delete Account.
  4. Re-authenticate if prompted (for security purposes).
  5. Confirm deletion. Your account and all personal data will be permanently removed.

After deletion, your personal information including your name, email address, and profile photo will no longer be accessible. Please note that this action is irreversible.

Need Assistance?

If you face any issues deleting your account, please contact us at: admin@bistudio.org
