About GoDutch

GoDutch helps friends, roommates, and travel groups split expenses easily. Track shared bills, see balances instantly, and make sure everyone pays their fair share.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

Open the app and create a group for your trip, event, or shared expenses.

How do I add an expense?

Inside a group tap the add expense button, enter the amount and participants. GoDutch automatically calculates balances.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app → Menu → Scroll down → Tap "Delete Account". Your personal data will be permanently removed.
