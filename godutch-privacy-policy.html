Go Dutch Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7 March 2026

Go Dutch respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage or delete your account.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we only collect the information necessary for account creation and profile management:

  • Name – provided by you during account creation.
  • Email address – provided by you or through Apple/Google sign-in.
  • Profile photo – if you choose to upload one.

We do not collect any other personal, financial, or sensitive information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your profile information in the app.
  • Allow you to use app features that require identification.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or other purposes.

3. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. When you delete your account, all personal data associated with your account, including your name, email, and profile photo, will be permanently removed.

To delete your account:

  1. Open the app and log in.
  2. Go to the Menu via the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and select Delete Account.
  4. Re-authenticate if prompted.
  5. Confirm deletion.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The most recent version will always be available in the app.
